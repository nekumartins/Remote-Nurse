Hypertension Risk Factors

Hypertension is a condition characterized by elevated blood pressure. The following factors contribute to hypertension risk:

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Chest Pain
  • Resting Blood Pressure
  • Cholesterol Level
  • Fasting Blood Sugar
  • Max Heart Rate
  • Exercise Induced Angina

Reducing Hypertension Risk

There are several steps that can be taken to reduce hypertension risk, including:

  • Regular exercise
  • Healthy diet
  • Weight management
  • Reducing sodium intake
  • Limiting alcohol consumption
  • Managing stress
  • Smoking cessation

About Us

Hypertension Detection is a website dedicated to helping individuals detect hypertension and reduce their risk of developing it. Our mission is to provide accurate and up-to-date information on hypertension risk factors and prevention strategies.
